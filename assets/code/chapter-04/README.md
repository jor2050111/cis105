## Data dictionary

**pitch-outline.docx / pitch-outline.rtf:** Saguaro Hall's approved pitch outline for a Phoenix small-business showcase: 9 slide titles styled Heading 1 and 32 bullets styled Heading 2, identical content in both files. The outline ships with three deliberate flaws the lab asks students to find and repair: one slide overloaded with nine bullets, one bullet written as a full spoken sentence that belongs in speaker notes, and the pricing slide placed before the slide that explains what a booking includes. Do not edit these files. Import them into PowerPoint and make every fix in the deck.
